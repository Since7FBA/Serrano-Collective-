Our Story

Serrano Collective is an independent retailer focused on high‑quality, practical goods for everyday life. We curate small batches from trusted makers and established brands—prioritizing durability, clean design, and fair value. Our goal is simple: reliable essentials you’ll use daily, shipped quickly and backed by friendly support.

Shipping Policy

  • Processing time: 1–2 business days.
  • US shipping: Free on orders $50+; calculated rates below $50.
  • Tracking provided via email for all orders.
  • Delivery estimates: 3–7 business days depending on destination.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Refunds issued within 5–10 business days after receipt.
  • Start a return: support@serranocollective.com.

Privacy Policy
