Our Story

Serrano Collective is a small, independent retailer focused on quality, utility, and timeless style. We partner with makers and brands that prioritize craftsmanship and responsible production, then bring those goods straight to you with fast shipping and friendly, transparent service. Every product in our shop is curated to elevate daily living.

Shipping Policy

  • Processing time: 1–2 business days.
  • US shipping: Free on orders $50+; calculated rates below $50.
  • Tracking provided via email for all orders.
  • Delivery estimates: 3–7 business days depending on destination.
  • Undeliverable packages may incur re‑shipment postage.

Return Policy

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Refunds issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after receipt.
  • Start a return by emailing support@serranocollective.com.

Privacy Policy

We collect only the information needed to process your order (name, email, shipping address, payment). Payments are processed securely by our PCI‑compliant provider; we never store full card details. We do not sell personal data. For any privacy requests, contact support@serranocollective.com.

Terms of Service

  • All prices are listed in USD.
  • Orders are subject to acceptance and availability.
  • We reserve the right to update policies and pricing at any time.
  • By purchasing, you agree to our Shipping, Return, and Privacy policies.
